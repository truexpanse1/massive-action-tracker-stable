# Sales Target Calculator - User Guide

## What This Tool Does

The **Massive Action Targets** calculator helps you turn a big money goal into a simple daily plan. It uses basic math to figure out exactly what you need to do every day to hit your annual revenue target.

### The Big Idea

Instead of guessing how many calls to make, this calculator works **backward** from your goal:

1. Start with how much money you want to make
2. Figure out how many sales you need
3. Calculate how many leads and opportunities required
4. Break it down into daily activities

**Result:** You get a clear daily plan that, if followed consistently, will get you to your goal.

## How to Use It

### Step 1: Your Money Goal

**Question 1: How much money do you want to make this year?**
- Enter your annual revenue goal
- Example: $500,000

**Question 2: How much is one sale worth?**
- Enter your average deal size or commission
- Example: $5,000 per customer

### Step 2: Your Time

**Question 3: How many days will you work this year?**
- Usually 250 days (5 days/week √ó 50 weeks)
- Adjust if you take more time off
- Example: 240 days if you take 3 weeks vacation

**Question 4: How many days does it take to close a sale?**
- From first contact to money in the bank
- Be realistic - include follow-up time
- Example: 30 days for most B2B sales, 45 days for real estate

### Step 3: Your Success Rates

**Question 5: Out of 100 leads, how many become real opportunities?**
- A "lead" is someone you contacted
- An "opportunity" is someone ready to buy
- Example: 25% (25 out of 100)

**Question 6: Out of 100 opportunities, how many buy?**
- Your close rate
- Be honest - this is critical
- Example: 25% (1 in 4 closes)

**üí° Don't Know Your Rates?**
If you're new or don't track these numbers:
- Start with 25% for both (industry average)
- Track your actual numbers for 30 days
- Come back and update the calculator

### Step 4: Your Activities

**For each lead, how many times do you...**

- **Call them?** (Usually 3 calls)
- **Email them?** (Usually 5 emails)
- **Text them?** (Usually 2 texts)

These are your activity ratios. Adjust based on your sales process.

### Step 5: Calculate

Click **"Show Me My Daily Plan"** and the calculator will show you:

1. **Your Daily Plan** - What to do every single day
2. **Weekly Targets** - What to hit each week
3. **Monthly Targets** - What to hit each month
4. **The Big Picture** - Your full year breakdown

#### üí∞ RESULTS (What You'll Make)

### Pipeline Health Metrics

**Coverage Ratio (e.g., 4x)**
- How much pipeline you need vs. your goal
- Example: 4x means you need $4 in pipeline for every $1 you want to close
- Lower close rate = higher coverage needed

**Required Pipeline Value**
- Total $ value your pipeline should have
- Example: $2M pipeline to hit $500k goal with 25% close rate

**Sales Velocity**
- How much money your pipeline generates per day
- Helps you forecast when deals will close

## Pro Tips

### 1. Do Calls First
Make all your calls before lunch. Don't check email or do other work first. Prospecting is your #1 priority.

### 2. Track Everything

- Leads ‚Üí Opportunities: Count how many leads became real prospects

## Common Questions

### Q: What if my numbers seem too high?

**A:** The calculator is showing you the **reality** of hitting your goal. You have three options:

1. **Do the work** - Hit those numbers every day
2. **Improve your conversion rates** - Get better at selling (less work needed)
3. **Lower your goal** - Be realistic about what you can achieve

Most people fail because they set big goals but don't do the daily work required.

### Q: What if I don't know my conversion rates?

**A:** Start with 25% for both (industry average). Track your numbers for 30 days, then update the calculator with your real rates.

### Q: Can I save my plan?

**A:** Yes! Take a screenshot of your daily plan or bookmark this page. In a future update, you'll be able to save multiple scenarios to the Resources section.

### Q: What if my sales cycle is longer than my working days?

**A:** That's normal! The calculator accounts for this with the **pipeline coverage ratio**. You need to build a pipeline now that will pay off later. Focus on adding leads and opportunities today, even if they won't close for 60-90 days.

### Q: Do I need to hit my daily deal target every day?

**A:** No! Deals don't close every day. Your daily deal target (like 0.4) means you need about 2 deals per week or 8-9 per month. Focus on your **activity targets** (calls, emails, leads) - those you CAN control daily.

### Q: What if I have a bad week?

**A:** One bad week won't kill you, but you need to make it up. If you miss your weekly target, you need to work harder next week. The calculator shows you the **minimum** required - doing more is always better.
